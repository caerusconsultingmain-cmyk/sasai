Spatial intelligence

When the job is physical, the data needs to understand place.

Spatial systems are where this becomes difficult to fake. If location, geometry, site state, or physical assets matter, the software has to work with the real world as it is.

01

Capture

Bring in imagery, LiDAR, site context, property boundaries, or other physical-world inputs.

02

Structure

Turn loose files and raw data into usable geometry, identity, and context.

03

Diagnose

Extract the signals that matter: area, condition, change, risk, access, overlap, or proximity.

04

Operate

Use that output in quoting, monitoring, field decisions, reports, or downstream automation.
